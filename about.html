About Us

At MIRINS, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive gynecological and obstetric care with compassion, innovation, and excellence. Our state-of-the-art facility is designed to cater to the unique healthcare needs of women.

Our Mission

To deliver personalized and exceptional healthcare services for women at every stage of life, ensuring dignity, empathy, and respect.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in women’s health, recognized for innovative care and a patient-first approach.

Our Values

  • Compassionate Care
  • Innovation and Excellence
  • Integrity and Respect
  • Teamwork and Collaboration
  • Patient-Centric Approach

Our History

Established in 2012, MIRINS has grown to become a trusted name in gynecology and obstetrics. With over two decades of service, we’ve supported thousands of women in their healthcare journeys.
